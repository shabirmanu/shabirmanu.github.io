Research Interests

LLM Optimization

I am focused on optimizing large language models (LLMs) to reduce their carbon footprint, which is a leading contributor to the global energy and water crises. The massive computational requirements of these models result in significant energy consumption and strain on natural resources, especially water, exacerbating these environmental challenges. My work aims to develop more efficient, eco-friendly algorithms and approaches to mitigate the environmental impact of AI technologies, ensuring that advancements in AI are not only innovative but also sustainable.
